Khichdi

Description

A simple and hearty dish made of dal andef basmati rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Chillis
  • Ghee
  • Bay leaf
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Garam masala
  • Chili powder
  • Asofoetida
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and lentils. Use them in equal parts of the recipe
  2. Melt the Ghee, add in your Spices and Chillis/
  3. Add the Lentils, Rice, Salt, COriander, Garam Masala, Bay Leaf and your choice of vegetables.
  4. Pour 4-5 cups of water into the pot and give everything a good stir. Bring the water to a boil, and then lower it a simmer. Stir the Khichdi every 5 or so minutes to allow the starches to move around, making it creamy.
  5. After about 25 minutes of simmering, the lentils and rice will have broken downm and the dish is ready to be served.
